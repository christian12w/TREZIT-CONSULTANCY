About Our Company

Learn more about Trezit Consultancy and General Dealers

Empowering Businesses with Strategic Solutions

Trezit Consultancy and General Dealers is a privately owned Zambian firm that delivers a unique combination of professional consultancy services and diversified trading solutions. Founded with the vision to address gaps in business development and supply chain efficiency, Trezit is committed to excellence, innovation, and integrity.

Headquartered in Lusaka, we serve a diverse clientele across sectors, including small and medium-sized enterprises (SMEs), non-governmental organizations (NGOs), government institutions, and private enterprises.

Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our services

What types of businesses do you work with?

We work with a diverse range of clients including small and medium-sized enterprises (SMEs), non-governmental organizations (NGOs), government institutions, and private enterprises across various industries in Zambia and Southern Africa.

How long does a typical consultancy project take?

Project duration varies depending on scope and complexity. A basic business strategy project might take 4-6 weeks, while larger initiatives could span several months. We provide a detailed timeline during our initial consultation.

Do you provide services outside of Zambia?

Yes, we provide consultancy and trading services throughout Southern Africa. Our team has experience working with clients in neighboring countries and can customize our approach to meet regional requirements.

What makes Trezit Consultancy different from competitors?

Our local expertise combined with international standards, commitment to sustainable development, and personalized approach set us apart. We focus on building long-term partnerships and delivering measurable results tailored to each client's unique needs.

How do you ensure data security and confidentiality?

We maintain strict confidentiality protocols and sign non-disclosure agreements as needed. All client data is securely stored and accessed only by authorized personnel. We comply with relevant data protection regulations.
